## Icon System

### Style Guidelines

- **Stroke Width**: 2px

- **Viewbox**: 24x24

## Voice & Tone

### Writing Style
- **Professional** but approachable
- **Clear** and easy to understand
- **Empathetic** and caring
- **Action-oriented** with clear CTAs
- **Accessible** language (avoid jargon)

### Example Phrases
✅ **Good**: "We're here to help you feel better"  
❌ **Avoid**: "Healthcare service delivery optimization"

✅ **Good**: "Schedule your appointment in minutes"  
❌ **Avoid**: "Utilize our appointment scheduling system"

✅ **Good**: "Questions? We're available 24/7"  
❌ **Avoid**: "For inquiries, consult our support documentation"

## Usage Guidelines

### Logo Usage

✅ **Do**:
- Use on white or light backgrounds
- Maintain clear space (minimum 16px)
- Scale proportionally
- Use official color versions

❌ **Don't**:
- Stretch or distort
- Change colors
- Add effects (shadows, outlines)
- Place on busy backgrounds
- Use low-resolution versions

### Brand Consistency

**Maintain consistency across**:
- Help Center
- Patient Portal
- Marketing materials
- Social media
- Physical locations
- Staff communications
